## 运行示例

在仓库根目录（`lambda_dpo`）下可直接运行以下命令：

- SFT：`llamafactory-cli train examples/train_full/llama3_8b_full_sft.yaml`
- Lambda DPO：`llamafactory-cli train examples/train_full/llama3_8b_full_lambdadpo.yaml`
- ORPO：`llamafactory-cli train examples/train_full/llama3_8b_full_orpo.yaml`
- SimPO：`llamafactory-cli train examples/train_full/llama3_8b_full_simpo.yaml`
- DPO：`llamafactory-cli train examples/train_full/llama3_8b_full_dpo.yaml`
